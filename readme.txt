Simple plug-in that displays your Twitter Followers & Rss Subscibers Stats. Just activate & setup this widget.

== Description ==

This plugin will help you to display your Twitter Followers & Rss Subscibers Stats.

As we increasingly depend on more and more social services, there rises the need to provide a simple way to let our website visitors take part of our diverse social presence. This plugin will help you to display your WordPress Blog/Site Twitter Followers & Rss Subscibers Stats at the desired sidebar in the WordPress Widgets.

<strong>How this plugin helps you to increase your Twitter Followers & Rss Subscibers?</strong>

* Show your visiters your Twitter Followers & Rss Subscibers Stats.
* According to your Twitter Followers & Rss Subscibers Stats visitors may like to Follow / Subscibers your WordPress Blog/Site.
* Increase your Twitter Followers & Rss Subscibers.


== Installation ==

Installation Steps

1. Upload the TwitterRSS Stats folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory via FTP. <strong>OR</strong> Add the TwitterRSS Plugin via the WordPress Plugins menu.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Add the Widget to the desired sidebar in the WordPress Widgets menu.
4. Insert your Twitter username (i.e webdesignergeek) and Feedburner ID (i.e webdesignergeeks) & Save
5. Rate the plugin and verify that it works at wordpress.org.
6. Leave a comment regarding bugs, feature request, Tea recipes at http://webdesignergeeks.com/cms/wordpress/twitterrss-stats-widget-how-to-create-wordpress-widget-plug-in-from-scratch/

Some Formatting Tips

* If you know CSS then you can change this widget colour  combination according to your WordPress Blog/Site. Go to WP Dashboard>Plugins>Editor> select TwitterRSS Stats > Edit TRR_Stats/css/style.css
